Activities

A hobby a day keeps the doldrums away. Good hobbies can be pursued as a leisure activity. Children need to be active every day to promote their healthy growth and development. Kids who establish healthy lifestyle patterns at a young age will carry them - and their benefits - forward for the rest of their lives.

Isha's Play School arranges various fun and interactive activities for the development of the students.

The Prime Activities are :

Isha's Abacus Center

A Mathematical Operational Tool Based Learning Procedure for strengthening mathematical calculations and concepts.

Isha's Junior English

Provides your child with knowledge about the most widely international language i.e. English at the early stage of learning. Also inproves the grammatical skills of your child.

Isha's Handwriting Improvement

Handwriting is an art which describes your personality immensely. To improve such an art, Isha's Eductaion Center has come up with Handwriting Improvement Coaching.

Isha's Summer Camp

Summer Camp is a great opportunity to explore new things or dive deep into areas you love. Isha's Play School presents Isha's Summer Camp for the Young Minds to discover the undiscovered skills and have fun.

Celebration of all the Festivals and imbibing Social and Religious Awareness among the kids is an important aspect which Isha's Play School thrives to achieve. Also various competitions held, encourage the young minds to imply their creative skills to a perfect use.

Social Service Acts

Isha Play School also inculcates the social responsibilities among the students throught various acts towards the service of the society.
Apulki School for Mentally Challenged Children is a center for mentally challenged or handicapped children. These kids are deprived from many forms of freedom and other basic rights.
Isha Play School provides these students a platform to showcase their talents among the normal society as well as donates generously to their center. Each year the students of Apulki School are invited to mingle with the students of Isha Play School during the annual gathering. The complete expenses for their travel as well as other essentials is compensated by the Play School.
